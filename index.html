Welcome to Starblade Realms

Quick tutorial:

• Move with WASD
• Aim with Mouse
• Attack with Space
• Dash with Shift
• Use Q when Super Meter is full
• Use potion with H or the Use Potion button
• Switch weapons anytime with 1-9 or weapon buttons
• Open backpack with B

You start with two weapons: Star Sword (melee, can deflect boss shots on timed swings) and Moon Bow (ranged).

Press Ready for Round when you want monsters to spawn. Every realm has 5 rounds, then a boss, and the game keeps going to higher realms.

Defeat bosses to unlock new weapons, and upgrade weapons in the panel. Weapons include short-range and long-range styles with different supers.
